CIET-NCERT conducts online training to promote use of technology in teaching and learning process. The National Education Policy – 2020 (NEP-2020) holds emphasis on the interplay of education and technology. The policy notes that one of the central principles to steer the education system will be the ‘extensive use of technology in teaching and learning, removing language barriers, increasing access as well as education planning and management’. In the light, NEP 2020 has set out laying standards to encourage digital content development, which will become a digital push for quality development in the field of Education.
