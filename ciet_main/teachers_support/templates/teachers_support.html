Teachers support material on Inclusive Education

The handbooks for teachers on Inclusive Education provide exemplary guidelines for meeting the special needs of children in an inclusive classroom. Successful inclusion practices highlight the significance of not only the presence of children in the class but also the quality of their achievement across the curriculum. The handbooks manuals and guidelines provide handholding for the teachers and guides them and other stakeholders towards future action.
