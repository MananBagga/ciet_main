Inclusion in education is inevitable and at present the education system in India has entered an era where after generating sufficient awareness about special/diverse learners and their learning needs, we are grappling with the challenge of the "how" and "what" of inclusive education pedagogy practices. To further the cause of equitable and inclusive education envisaged in the National Education Policy 2020, CIET-NCERT has initiated a live interaction series, aiming at the clientele group of 12 DTH channels under PM eVidya (one class one channel).
