Department of ICT & Training

Welcome to the Department of Information and Communication Technologies, CIET. Constituent broadly works for:

  • Creation of multimedia materials for students and teachers
  • Imparting training on Use of ICTs in teaching-learning process and Research Methodology for ICTs in Education etc. for teachers and teacher-educators i.e. schools, DIETs, CTEs, IASES, SIETs, SCERTs/SIEs, faculty from Education Departments of Indian Universities.
  • Faculty training on ICTs in Education including Web 2.0 Tools, Wiki/WikiEducator, MacLab and Robotino. Development and Use of Open Source Materials etc.
  • Innovations in ICTs in Education.
  • Dissemination and extension of ICT resources among schools, students and teachers in every nook and corner of the country is the motto of the Division.
  • Continuous updating and maintenance of CIET website.
