Planning & Research Division

The division looks after implementing and monitoring all the academic programmes duly approved by the Institutional Advisory Board (IAB) and PAC-NCERT, PAB of DSEL, MOE-GOI, and other projects funded by National/International funding agencies. Additionally, we collaborate with MOE and other State Institutes of Educational Technology (SIETs) for projects in the field of educational technology. With strategic planning, programme implementation, and rigorous monitoring the PRD department of CIET has created a niche in research and specialized training in educational technology.
