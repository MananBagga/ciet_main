Programme Advisory Committee (PAC) of National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) is an academic body which looks into the academic aspects of the programme proposed by different constituent units of NCERT. The PAC will only examine development, training, and extension programmes. The list of PAC programmes are listed below with their dedicated programme codes and point of contact.
